Exploring the Tennis gender pay gap in 2019

Although they are among the highest-paid professional female athletes, female tennis players haven't yet reached salary equity. This visualization explores the earnings distribution of the top 50 women and the top 50 men tennis players in 2019. Only the prize money won on tour has been taken into account, leaving aside endorsement deals.
